Decoding Social Security Numbers in One Step
(for numbers issued prior to June 25, 2011)

Stephen P.Morse, San Francisco



This page is involved with decoding social security numbers (SSNs) in order to find out when and where they were issued.
If you came here from a page that talks about restoring your credit rating, you are in the wrong place.
Please don't ask me any questions about fixing credit ratings, obtaining new SSNs, or obtaining CPNs (whatever that is).

Three-Digit Decoder

SSN starting with - XX - XXXX
was issued in
Where card was issued, not where person was born



Five-Digit Decoder

SSN starting with - - XXXX
was issued in 602-43: California, 2003-2004
Where and when card was issued, not where and when person was born
